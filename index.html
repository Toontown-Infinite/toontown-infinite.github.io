Toontown Infinite is Closed.


Background information

In early April, we evaluated the future of Toontown Infinite. We believed that the community created around our small private server was great, and that it had a lot to offer. However, we came to the unfortunate realization that while we had a loyal set of players, there wasnâ€™t much internal interest in continuing the development of the game. Additionally, we had been having issues securing funding for servers to keep the project alive.


Sad news

With all of this considered, we made the hard decision to stop all development on Infinite, and to close down the project. We know that this can be hard to hear, especially for players that have invested time into the game. To help ease the transition, the server was kept live until April 13th so that our players could make an easier transition over to the server of their choice.


I can pay for the servers though

It doesn't matter. Even if someone were willing to donate money, we already said we're going through with shutting down the servers, and that doesn't change our interest in developing and supporting Toontown.

Will you open source the game

No. The community doesn't need a dozen spin-offs. The only open source version will be the one from January 2015. Play Toontown Rewritten or come up with an original idea for the game, but please don't just rehost ours.

However, the website is open sourced here. I hope someone can learn from it [also note that the site was never fully finished, and i'm not happy with it].


Will we ever see a return

No. All data on the servers (accounts, toons, support tickets, etc.) will be destroyed after closing. We do not want to see another reincarnation of the game.


What are you going to do with toontowninfinite.com

I don't know. It's registered practically for another year, until March 2017. Send me an email: brandon@toontowninfinite.com. Maybe we can work something out.


Closing notes

We want to recognize the work of everyone on the project that made this game great, and thank them for their hard work. And of course, we would like to thank everyone who has played Infinite over the last couple years. Without you, there would have been no Infinite.
